ABOUT



Aim: Performing short circuit test on single phase transformer to calculate:


    1.Copper losses of the transformer
    2.Circuit parameters of the transformer [Resistance, Reactance and Impedence].


Apparatus:

S.No Name Specification
01 Transformer 1KvA
02 Auto-transformer 230v/0-270v
03 voltmeter 0-30v
04 Ammeter 0-5A
05 Watt Meter 0-75w


Theory:


   1. A single phase 1KVA, 230 V/115 V step down transformer is used to determine core and copper losses. This works on the principle of mutual induction.


   2. The primary side is High Voltage side consists of 0-230 V and the secondary side is Low Voltage side consists of 0-115 V.


   3. This transformer converts high voltage, low current power into low voltage, high current power.


   4. It is used to step down the voltage from 230V/115V.
